Explore the Sahyadri

Sahyadri_Navigator is a trekking management web application that enhance the management of trekking activities. The system aims to provide users-Trekking Club to post the trekks and for trekkers to book the upcoming trekks of their dream life. We also provide the facility of User-Friendly UI to the users for a much more hassle-free experience by using a microservices architecture and following the Agile methodology.
